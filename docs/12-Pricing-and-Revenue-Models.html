12 Pricing And Revenue Models

End of pricing guide.

1. Community Tier

  • Price: Free
  • Seats: Up to 3
  • API Calls: 1 M calls/month
  • Support: Community forum, GitHub issues
  • Purpose: Drive adoption, feedback & evangelism

2. Paid Tiers

Professional

  • Pricing: $75/user/month (billed annually at $900/user/year)
  • Seats: Up to 20
  • API Calls: 10 M calls/month
  • Support: Email support (9×5)
  • Trial: 14-day full-feature trial (max 5 seats)

Enterprise

  • Pricing: $10 000/month base + $50/user/month
  • Seats & Calls: Unlimited
  • Support: SLA-backed (24×5), private registry, audit logs
  • Trial: 30-day POC with unlimited seats

Platinum

  • Pricing: Custom ≥ $50 000/month
  • Seats & Calls: Unlimited
  • Support: Dedicated CSM, on-prem deployment, security review, 24×7 SLA
  • Trial: 30-day POC + dedicated onboarding

3. Trial Terms

  • Credit card required at signup
  • Usage cap: 10 M calls total during trial
  • Notifications: Day 7 & Day 1 reminders
  • Conversion: One-click upgrade preserves settings & artifacts
